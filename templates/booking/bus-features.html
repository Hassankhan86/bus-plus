Bus Features

At Buses Plus, your comfort is our top priority. We strive to create a home-away-from-home experience for all our travelers. Our buses are equipped with a range of fantastic features to ensure you can unwind and relax throughout your journey.

Out Side The Bus

Eco-friendly engines

At Buses Plus, we are dedicated to environmental stewardship and believe in reducing our carbon footprint. That's why every Buses Plus bus is equipped with state-of-the-art technology that burns fuel cleanly, ensuring we play our part in preserving the environment.

Under-bus baggage compartment

When you travel with us, you can carry one carry-on bag with you onboard, and we provide the convenience of storing up to two additional bags under the bus. So, you can bring along all your essentials without any worries.our baggage details page..

Outstanding safety record

We've received the highest possible safety rating from the Department of Transportation every year for more than ten years. Drivers go through rigorous training and check the bus before each trip. More about our training.

Wheelchair lift

If you're using a wheelchair or mobility scooter, all of our buses are equipped with a wheelchair lift to help you get on board.

In Side the Bus

Wheelchair space

At Buses Plus,We can accommodate two passengers traveling in a wheelchair or mobility scooter (we remove seats to make plenty of space for you). Learn how we accommodate customers with disabilities. .

Air conditioning

Air conditioning helps to keep the bus at a comfortable temperature, but you'll also have individual air outlets at your seat that you can adjust.

Overhead storage

You can free up more room around you by putting your carry-on baggage in the storage compartment above your seat.

Choose your seat

Now, you can choose your preferred seat when you book your ticket. All customers will be assigned a seat, but you have the option to choose your preferred seat. The seat reservation fee varies based on the route.

On-board restroom

At Buses Plus, we prioritize the comfort and well-being of our passengers throughout their journey. We understand the importance of rest during long trips, and that's why we make scheduled stops for rest breaks along the way.

At Your Seat

Individual power outlets

Nearly every seat has its own standard power outlet, so you can keep your devices charged all the way to your destination. Getting off with a fully charged phone is extra handy if you're navigating around a new place.

Extra legroom

At Buses Plus, we value your comfort above all else. That's why we provide extra legroom on all our buses, ensuring you have plenty of space to stretch out and relax during your journey.

Free Wi-Fi

Wi-Fi is free on all our buses, for all passengers. We see it as a must-have these days, not a premium feature. So go ahead, check your emails or Instagram window scenery from the road (we'd love if you tag it #gobusesplus). Please don't stream video during your journey though - we've all got to share the bandwidth!

Reclining leather seats

Our comfy leather seats are adjustable and have a handy footrest, so you can find just the right spot to relax. Once you're settled, get ready to count some sheep.

3-point safety belts

All of our new buses have three-point safety belts (that's most of our fleet). They have both shoulder and lap harnesses and you can use them with standard car child seats.

No middle seats

On our buses, everyone gets a window or an aisle seat. So, you don't need to worry about getting squashed in the middle and having to fight for arm rests.
