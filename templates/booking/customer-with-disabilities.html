Customers with Disabilities

Buses Plus complies with ADA requirements and is dedicated to serving people with disabilities. For any inquiries, concerns, or complaints related to accessible travel, please reach out to us using the contact information provided below.

Purchase & Travel Assistance:

At Buses Plus, we prioritize the comfort and needs of passengers with disabilities. Our team is committed to providing a comfortable journey for everyone on board. If you have a disability, please don't hesitate to seek assistance from our staff at each location, including different drivers if you have multiple legs of your trip. We are here to ensure your travel experience with Buses Plus is enjoyable and accommodating. Your satisfaction is our utmost priority, and we're ready to help in any way we can. If you are having trouble purchasing tickets electronically due to a disability, one of our Customer Service Representatives will be happy to help you by phone at 1800818816 and waive the standard convenience fee after confirming that your inability to book electronically is due to your disability. You may also email Greyhound if you are having problems due to your disability: info@busesplus.com.au. Here are a few additional number that might also be useful:
