Payment and ticket options

Ready to book a trip? You have several ways to buy your Buses Plus tickets, with lots of flexible payment options.

Online


Payment options: Debit/Credit card, cash or Stripe


Ticket options:
  • Buy online and show your ticket on your phone or print your ticket at home.
  • Reserve your ticket online via our website or mobile app and pay with cash at over terminals.

    • At the station


    Payment options: Debit/Credit card, or cash (Different forms of payment cannot be combined)

    Pay in person at the station:

  • You can buy and print your ticket at a self-service kiosk.
  • You can buy a ticket for travel the same day, or take advantage of lower prices by buying your ticket in advance (although it still won't be as low as buying in advance online).

    • Other Location


    Payment options: Debit or credit card

    Ticket options:

    Buy your ticket at one of the thousands of independent agents (like convenience stores and gas stations) across the Australia.
